About me

MSc candidate engineering student at Bogazici University. I got my Civil Engineering degree from MEF University in 2019. Don't be afraid, I enjoy coding and developing more than dealing with concrete.

During my time in master’s degree, I enrolled in a nation-wide government research and development project about classifying a domestically produced engineering material and I did the planning, conducting and analysing phases for Bogazici University. I am the first researcher in Turkey who completed specification level structural tests on any Turkish Timber Species. I am proud to say that maybe one day, my project will help to reduce carbon emission throughout the world.

Last semester, I took PhD level courses such as Data Mining and Deep Neural Networks and developed several projects in these topics, which you can see in my GitHub or Portfolio page. These projects are real life data-based projects where I was expected to gather insights to develop representations, making forecasts on several datasets, help them to visualize and reported properly.

Since my academic researcher identity is combined with my passion in coding, I really enjoy following software trends in daily life and digesting the beginning, development, and future processes of these trends.
